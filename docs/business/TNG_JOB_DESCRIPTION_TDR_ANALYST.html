Job Description
Epic TDR Analyst
Department: Consulting Services Reports To: Project Manager FLSA Status: Non-Exempt Effective: February 2026
Position Summary: The Epic TDR (Technical Dress Rehearsal) Analyst supports healthcare organizations during Epic EHR implementations by performing detailed validation, testing, and troubleshooting of data conversions, interfaces, and workflow configurations. This role is critical to ensuring data integrity and system readiness for go-live.
Employment TypeW-2 Employee, Project-Based
Work LocationClient Site (Onsite Required)
TravelUp to 100% during engagement
ScheduleFull-time; may include evenings/weekends during go-live

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

Data Validation and Testing

  • Review and validate extracted source system data for accuracy, completeness, and format compliance
  • Perform data reconciliation between source systems and Epic environment
  • Execute test scripts and document results for data conversion validation
  • Identify, document, and track data quality issues and discrepancies
  • Validate migrated records in Epic against source documentation

Interface Testing

  • Test inbound and outbound interfaces between Epic and ancillary systems
  • Validate HL7 message processing and data mapping accuracy
  • Document interface testing results and escalate failures
  • Coordinate with integration teams to resolve interface issues

Workflow Validation

  • Execute end-to-end workflow testing scenarios
  • Validate clinical and revenue cycle workflows function as designed
  • Identify workflow gaps and configuration issues
  • Support user acceptance testing activities

Issue Management

  • Document defects with detailed reproduction steps
  • Triage and prioritize issues based on go-live impact
  • Partner with build teams to resolve identified issues
  • Track issue resolution and verify fixes

Go-Live Support

  • Support cutover readiness activities and dress rehearsals
  • Participate in command center operations during go-live
  • Provide real-time troubleshooting and issue resolution

Qualifications

Required

  • Bachelor's degree in Healthcare, Information Technology, or related field; or equivalent experience
  • Minimum 2 years experience in healthcare IT or EHR implementations
  • Experience with Epic systems or comparable EHR platforms
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent attention to detail and data accuracy
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to travel up to 100% and work extended hours during critical phases

Preferred

  • Epic certification in one or more modules
  • Experience with TDR, go-live, or cutover activities
  • Knowledge of HL7 interfaces and data integration
  • Experience with SQL or data analysis tools
  • Clinical background (RN, LPN, HIM)
