## OFFER LETTER TEMPLATE

```
[NICEHR LETTERHEAD]

[DATE]

Dear [CONSULTANT NAME],

I am pleased to offer you a position with THE NICEHR GROUP, LLC ("Company") as
[POSITION TITLE] for our [CLIENT NAME] [PROJECT NAME] Project. This position
will report to [MANAGER NAME].

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ENGAGEMENT DETAILS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Project:              [CLIENT NAME] - [PROJECT NAME]
Location:             [CITY, STATE]
Travel Date:          [DATE]
Orientation Date:     [DATE]
Start Date:           [DATE]
Anticipated End Date: [DATE]
Module/Role:          [EPIC MODULE / ROLE DESCRIPTION]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMPENSATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You will be classified as an hourly, non-exempt W-2 employee.

HOURLY RATES:
  - Orientation/Training Rate:  $[XX.00]/hour
  - Support/Billable Rate:      $[XX.00]/hour
  - Overtime Rate (>40 hrs):    $[XX.00]/hour (1.5x Support Rate)

OVERTIME: For any support billable hours worked over 40 hours per week, your
overtime rate will be [1.5x SUPPORT RATE]. All overtime hours must be
pre-approved by [MANAGER NAME]. Unapproved overtime may not be compensated.

PAYROLL: You will be paid [bi-weekly/weekly] in accordance with Company payroll
procedures, less applicable deductions.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PER DIEM & EXPENSES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Your per diem tier is determined by your distance from the project location:

[ ] TIER 1 - Remote (>50 miles from project site)
    Per Diem: $[240.00]/day
    - Eligible on all days engaged including travel in/out and orientation
    - Consultant books and pays for own accommodations
    - No expense receipts required for per diem
    - Travel day per diem requires arrival before [5:00 PM] local time

[ ] TIER 2 - Regional (11-50 miles from project site)
    Per Diem: $[175.00]/day
    - Per diem eligible only on days with 3+ hours worked
    - Per diem eligible on travel-out day (within 1 day of offboarding)
    - No expense receipts required for per diem

[ ] TIER 3 - Local (<11 miles from project site)
    Per Diem: $[75.00]/day OR No per diem
    - [Specify terms]

The tier designated in this offer letter will be used for the duration of the
project. If you have questions about your tier assignment, contact
[EMAIL ADDRESS].

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MANDATORY ONBOARDING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

There will be a MANDATORY onboarding session on [DATE] from [TIME] to [TIME].
All team members are required to register and check in during this event.

REQUIREMENTS:
- You must provide valid government-issued photo identification at check-in
- Your travel MUST be scheduled to arrive before [TIME] local time
- Failure to attend onboarding may result in release from the project
- Late arrival (after stated time) will disqualify you from travel day per diem

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONTINGENCIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This offer is contingent upon successful completion of:

1. Background/Reference Check
2. Drug Screening (if required by Client)
3. Occupational Health Requirements (immunizations, TB test, etc.)
4. Client Approval
5. Agreement to terms and conditions in this letter

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Your employment with the Company is for no specified period and constitutes
at-will employment. As a result, you are free to resign at any time, for any
reason or for no reason. Similarly, the Company is free to terminate its
employment relationship with you at any time, with or without cause, and with
or without notice.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EARLY TERMINATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If early employment termination from the project occurs due to:

a) YOUR VOLUNTARY TERMINATION (without 14 days written notice):
   - You forfeit final per diem payment
   - You are responsible for all travel costs to return home
   - Hourly rate for completed work will be paid at orientation rate

b) POOR PERFORMANCE OR MISREPRESENTATION OF COMPETENCY:
   - You are responsible for all travel costs to return home
   - Hourly rate for completed work will be paid at orientation rate
   - You may be ineligible for future Company engagements

c) CLIENT CANCELLATION OR PROJECT REDUCTION:
   - You will be paid for all hours worked through termination date
   - Per diem will be paid through last day worked plus travel-out day

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
NON-SOLICITATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

As a condition of employment, you agree that during your employment and for
twelve (12) months following termination, you shall not:

a) Directly recruit or solicit for employment any current employee of the
   Company or seek to persuade any employee to discontinue employment with
   the Company;

b) Solicit or encourage any Client of the Company to terminate or diminish
   its relationship with the Company;

c) Solicit business from any Client with which you had direct contact during
   your employment with the Company for the benefit of a directly competitive
   business.

NOTE: This is a non-solicitation agreement, NOT a non-compete. You remain free
to work in the healthcare IT industry and accept positions with other companies,
provided you do not directly solicit Company employees or clients.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONFIDENTIALITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

During your employment, you will have access to Confidential Information of the
Company, its Clients, and Affiliates. You agree that you will not use or
disclose to any person any Confidential Information obtained during your
employment, except as required for proper performance of your duties.

Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:
- Client information, systems, and data
- Patient health information (PHI)
- Company business strategies and client relationships
- Pricing, rates, and financial information
- Proprietary processes and methodologies

This confidentiality obligation continues after termination of employment.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WORK PRODUCT & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

All work product, documentation, training materials, workflows, and deliverables
created during your engagement are the sole property of the Client and Company.
You retain no rights to work created during the engagement.

You represent that you will not:
- Use proprietary information from previous employers
- Incorporate third-party intellectual property without written permission
- Retain copies of Client or Company materials after engagement ends

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FLOW-DOWN REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You agree to comply with all Client requirements, policies, and procedures that
the Company is obligated to flow down, including but not limited to:

- HIPAA compliance and training requirements
- Client background check and credentialing requirements
- Drug screening requirements (if applicable)
- Confidentiality and security obligations
- Badge and access control protocols
- Dress code and professional conduct standards
- IT security and acceptable use policies

Failure to comply with flow-down requirements is grounds for immediate
termination from the engagement.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
INDEMNIFICATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You agree to indemnify and hold harmless the Company from any claims, damages,
losses, or liabilities arising from:

- Your negligence, misconduct, or violation of law
- Your misrepresentation of qualifications or competency
- Your breach of confidentiality obligations
- Your violation of Client policies or procedures
- Your unauthorized disclosure of PHI or Confidential Information

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
IMMIGRATION COMPLIANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

For purposes of federal immigration law, you are required to provide documentary
evidence of your identity and eligibility for employment in the United States.
Such documentation must be provided within three (3) business days of your date
of hire, or your employment may be terminated.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMPANY POLICIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

As a Company employee, you will be expected to abide by all Company rules and
standards. You will be required to sign an acknowledgment that you have read
and understand our policies, which will be provided to you.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GOVERNING LAW & DISPUTE RESOLUTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of [STATE].

Any dispute arising from this agreement shall be resolved as follows:
1. Good faith negotiation between the parties (30 days)
2. If unresolved, binding arbitration in [CITY, STATE]

The prevailing party in any dispute shall be entitled to reasonable attorney's
fees and costs.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GENERAL PROVISIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. This letter constitutes the entire agreement and supersedes any prior
   agreements or representations regarding your employment.

2. This letter may not be modified except by written agreement signed by the
   Chief Executive Officer of the Company and you.

3. If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain
   in full effect.

4. The Company's failure to enforce any provision shall not constitute a
   waiver of that provision.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ACCEPTANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This offer of employment will terminate if not accepted, signed, and returned
by midnight Eastern Time on [DEADLINE DATE].

To accept this offer, please sign and date below.

We are excited to have you join our team and look forward to a successful
engagement!

Sincerely,


_________________________________

[TITLE]
THE NICEHR GROUP, LLC
```
