ACCEPTABLE FORMS OF IDENTIFICATION
Form I-9 Employment Eligibility Verification
IMPORTANT: All employees must present original, unexpired documents to verify identity and employment authorization within 3 business days of their start date. Photocopies are not acceptable. Documents must be presented in person to an HR representative.

Option 1: List A Documents (Identity AND Work Authorization)

Present ONE document from List A:
  • U.S. Passport or U.S. Passport Card
  • Permanent Resident Card (Form I-551, "Green Card")
  • Foreign passport with temporary I-551 stamp or printed notation
  • Employment Authorization Document (Form I-766)
  • Foreign passport with Form I-94 bearing same name and endorsement of nonimmigrant status with work authorization
  • Passport from Federated States of Micronesia (FSM) or Republic of Marshall Islands (RMI) with Form I-94 or I-94A

— OR —

Option 2: List B + List C Documents (Identity AND Work Authorization)

Present ONE document from List B (Identity):
  • Driver's license or state-issued ID card with photo
  • ID card issued by federal, state, or local government with photo
  • School ID card with photo
  • Voter registration card
  • U.S. Military card or draft record
  • Military dependent's ID card
  • U.S. Coast Guard Merchant Mariner Document (MMD)
  • Native American tribal document with photo
  • Driver's license issued by Canadian government

For persons under 18 unable to present above documents: school record, clinic/doctor/hospital record, or day-care/nursery school record

AND ONE document from List C (Work Authorization):
  • U.S. Social Security card (unrestricted, without employment restrictions)
  • Original or certified copy of U.S. birth certificate with official seal
  • Original or certified copy of birth certificate from U.S. territory
  • Certification of Birth Abroad (Form FS-545)
  • Certification of Report of Birth (Form DS-1350)
  • U.S. Citizen ID Card (Form I-197)
  • ID Card for Use of Resident Citizen in the U.S. (Form I-179)
  • Employment authorization document issued by DHS

Note: Social Security cards marked "NOT VALID FOR EMPLOYMENT" are NOT acceptable

Most Common Document Combinations

Employee Type Recommended Option Documents
U.S. Citizen List A (easiest) U.S. Passport
U.S. Citizen List B + C Driver's License + Social Security Card
U.S. Citizen List B + C State ID + Birth Certificate
Permanent Resident List A (easiest) Green Card (Form I-551)
Permanent Resident List B + C Driver's License + Green Card
Work Visa Holder List A Employment Authorization Document (EAD)

Important Reminders

Requirement Details
Deadline Documents must be presented within 3 business days of start date
Originals Only Photocopies, faxes, or scanned copies are NOT acceptable
Unexpired All documents must be current and unexpired at time of presentation
In-Person Documents must be presented in person; remote verification not permitted
No Receipts Receipts are only acceptable for lost/stolen/damaged documents (90-day limit)
Employee Choice Employers cannot specify which documents to present; employee chooses
